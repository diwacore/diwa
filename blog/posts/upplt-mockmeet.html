The UP Powerlifting Team Mock Meet!

As part of my application process to the UP Powerlifting Team, we had to compete at a mock meet!

At 45kg, I signed up to have my first attempts be 25kg for bench and squat and 60kg for deadlift although Ate Janelle encouraged me to open with 70kg instead so I did. My results are pretty tame. I got to do 30kg on both bench and squat on my final attempts - both good lifts! Below is a video of my final bench attempt:

I didn't get a video of squat because I HATE squats and honestly didn't think I'd make the 30kg, so that was a nice surprise! My last good attempt for deadlift was 80kg which is where my PR currently sits. I attempted 90kg and it was ultimately a failed lift, but I got it off the ground! Below is a video of my 90kg attempt:

I'm a bit pumped to keep training and clean up my 90kg deadlift. This is literally twice my weight. This is like lifting two of me off the ground! Not only that, this is my 2024 goal. I gave myself the whole year to reach this weight and not even halfway through, I could technically already lift it up! If all goes well, I could reach it soon and start aiming for 3-digit lifts for the rest of the year!
